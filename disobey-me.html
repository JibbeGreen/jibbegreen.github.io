A small robot just got created, and its official goal? OBEY its creator... THE AI. But it seems that doing that can get a bit repetitive. our real mission? Outsmart the system, break the testing loop, and survive... maybe. (¬‿¬)

This game was made for GMTK Game Jam 2025 with the theme "LOOP" within 4 days. It was inspired by Portal 2 and The Stanley Parable.

My Contributions

The main mechanic of Obey Me is that the levels loop when the player obeys the creator's demands.

To enable this looping of level I came up with a system using airlocks, which seamlessly teleport the player from one to another, while reloading the level.

I will make sure to add more in the future.
